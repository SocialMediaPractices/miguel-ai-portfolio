## Filtering rules

Good fit if the post asks for:
- LLM APIs, AI tools, prompt/instruction design
- Workflow automation, internal tools, CRM integrations
- Vercel/GitHub/lightweight web apps
- Sales/recruiting/ops/back-office automation
- Dashboards, KPI tracking, process mapping
- Human review, permissions, privacy/security boundaries

Lower fit if the post requires:
- Senior Python/JS engineering as the core requirement
- Production ML/MLOps, model training, Kubernetes, heavy backend ownership
- 5–8+ years dedicated automation engineering
- Full-time senior RevOps admin with Salesforce admin certification required
